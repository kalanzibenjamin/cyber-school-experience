Getting Started

Cyber School Experience is a student-powered learning community for Nursing (CN) and Midwifery (CM) students. We provide a central platform for study materials, academic updates, WhatsApp group connections, and peer support.

Visit our Chats page, find your class group, and click the "Join Group" button. You'll be redirected to WhatsApp where you can join the group directly.

Yes! Cyber School Experience is completely free for all Nursing and Midwifery students. We believe in accessible education for everyone.

Academics & Resources

We provide a variety of resources including:

  • Lecture notes and summaries
  • Past exam papers with solutions
  • Practice questions and quizzes
  • Clinical study guides
  • Recommended reading lists

All exam-related announcements are posted on our Announcements page and shared in the relevant WhatsApp groups. Make sure you're in your class group to receive timely updates.

Yes! We have weekly study groups for various subjects. Check the Announcements page for upcoming study sessions or ask in your WhatsApp group for study partners.

Community & Support

Cyber School Experience is open to all current students enrolled in Nursing (CN) and Midwifery (CM) certificate programs. Alumni are also welcome to stay connected and mentor current students.

There are many ways to contribute:

  • Share study materials and notes
  • Answer questions in WhatsApp groups
  • Lead study sessions
  • Become a group moderator
  • Share feedback and suggestions

We maintain a respectful and inclusive environment. Key guidelines include:

  • Be respectful and kind to all members
  • Share helpful and relevant content
  • Protect everyone's privacy
  • No spam or promotional content
  • Report issues to moderators

Read the full Chat Guidelines for more details.

Technical Support

If you're having trouble joining a group:

  • Make sure you have WhatsApp installed
  • Check that the link hasn't expired
  • Try opening the link in your browser first
  • Contact a moderator for a new link

You can report any issues to your group moderators or contact us through the Contact page. We'll get back to you as soon as possible.
