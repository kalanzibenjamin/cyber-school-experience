Chat Etiquette & Rules

Guidelines to ensure a positive and productive learning environment for all.

Our WhatsApp groups are designed to be safe, supportive spaces for Nursing (CN) and Midwifery (CM) students. By following these simple guidelines, we can all contribute to a positive learning community.

  • Be Respectful and Inclusive Treat every member with kindness and respect. We're all here to learn and grow together.
  • Share Helpful Information Contribute useful resources, study tips, and relevant announcements. Avoid spam or unrelated content.
  • Keep Discussions Relevant Stay on topic. Focus on academic support, learning resources, and community building.
  • Protect Privacy Do not share personal contact details publicly. Respect the privacy of all group members.
  • Be Considerate of Time Post during reasonable hours and avoid excessive messaging that might overwhelm others.
  • Report Issues If you see inappropriate behavior, notify the group moderators or admins immediately.

Do's

  • Ask questions and share knowledge
  • Encourage and support fellow students
  • Share helpful study materials
  • Use clear and respectful language
  • Reach out to moderators for help

Don'ts

  • Don't share spam or promotional content
  • Don't bully or harass others
  • Don't share exam answers (integrity matters)
  • Don't post personal contact info publicly
  • Don't send irrelevant or off-topic messages

Remember: These groups are here to help you succeed. By following these guidelines, you contribute to a positive and productive learning environment for everyone.
